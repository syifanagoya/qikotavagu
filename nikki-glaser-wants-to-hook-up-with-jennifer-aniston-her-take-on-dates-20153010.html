Nikki Glaser Wants to Hook Up With Jennifer Aniston: Her Take on Dates

Laugh with Us! Comedian Nikki Glaser recently stopped by Us Weekly‘s headquarters, where she shared her very hilarious take on dating, sending sexy photos, and more.

Glaser, who recently teamed up with Orbit gum for a blind dating promotion, reveals that she was once on dating apps.

“Tinder, I signed up for when it first came out,” she tells Us. But it got out of hand when fans realized who she was. “For whatever reason you don’t want to be recognized on those things. Even though everyone does it,” she continues.

The star, who’s best friends with Amy Schumer, had some outrageous sex secrets to spill about her pals — without naming names, of course. “My friend recently had a foursome with some people she recently met on a show,” Glaser reveals. She was onstage and was like, ‘This guy’s hot.’ And then they talked to her afterwards and were like, ‘We’re here to have a threesome, do you want to join us?’ So, she had a foursome.'”

Glaser opens up to Us about her own personal celebrity picks for potential romps. “[Jennifer] Aniston, I’m not gay,” she replies. “For her, I really would be… Zac Efron.” Her final pick was none other than John Stamos, who she claims she made “eye contact with” at some point.

The comic also had some funny things to say about sending racy snaps. “I have taken many in my day, and my day was yesterday,” Glaser tells Us. “I make sure I’m okay with the whole world seeing it.”
