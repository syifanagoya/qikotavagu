Early Life

Steve Austin was born as Steven James Anderson in Austin, Texas on December 18, 1964, to his parents Beverley Anderson and James Anderson. He later adopted his stepfather’s surname and became Steven James Williams.

Career

In 2003, he became the co-general manager of Raw. Since then he has made several appearances in the WWE in guest appearances.

Austin has also appeared in many films. He began acting when he was still a professional wrestler.
