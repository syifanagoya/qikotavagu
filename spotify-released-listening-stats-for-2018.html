Spotify Released Listening Stats For 2018

August 31, 2024
blog

Every year, Spotify releases statistics that contain what everyone listened to over the year including podcasts, playlists, albums, artists, and more. Can you guess who made the list?

The top artists of this year were Drake and Ariana Grande.

The most streamed song goes to Gods Plan by Drake.

The top breakout artists were Juice WRLD, Normani and King Princess.
