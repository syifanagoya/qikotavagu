All That Cast Reunited at Comic Con: Photos

August 28, 2024
blog

This is All That! The cast of Nickelodeon’s ‘90s sketch-comedy show reunited at New York’s Comic Con on Saturday, Oct. 10, and it was everything.

Kel Mitchell, Danny Tamberelli, Josh Server, and Lori Beth Denberg came together at the event and offered behind-the-scenes insights into the beloved show. They also talked about their auditions and their favorite moments from the show.

The stars even recreated some of their own skits from the beloved show, which ran from 1994 to 2005.

“Ever wonder what happened to the big ear of corn?” Tamberelli said on Vine after the panel, referring to one of the characters he played on the show. “We have to carry his babies #allthatreunion.”

Server, meanwhile, shared a video on Instagram of the venue full of fans and expressed his gratitude for their support. “All the fans and wonderful people attending comicon today, thank you for your continuous support over the years,” he wrote.

Mitchell also shared a clip in which he and his castmates said, “Whoa, who’s that?! What’s up?!”

The actor, 37, recently told Us Weekly exclusively that a sequel to his 1997 Nickelodeon comedy Good Burger — which also starred original All That cast member Kenan Thompson — could be happening.

“We are both still super excited about it, we’ve been texting each other back and forth about it,” the comedian tells Us. “Kenan and I both were just cracking up.”
